Human Desease Detection Using Walking Video Analysis




Goal: The goal of this analysis is to classify normal and abnormal movements. The clue may be that while walking through a line the deviation of a key point of a normal person is different from an abnormal person. Using this principle we can differentiate normal people and abnormal people.

Possible Solutions: We can develop a Machine Learning model which can predict whether a personâ€™s movement is normal or abnormal. To do this we need to do the following steps. From the video, we will do these tasks:

  • Calculate the deviation from the white line from every frame of the video
  • Make a visualization from the deviation values by graph
  • We will collect videos from both normal people and sick people and make and dataset of the deviation graphs
  • A Deep learning model can be developed by the dataset.

Challenges:

  • Dataset collection
  • The line detection

Requirements:

  • A line should be drawn in the middle of the camera screen.
  • While capturing the video the line should be matched with the white line on the floor

Analysis of a video:

  • Keypoints Detection
  • Find the deviation of every key points from the Line
  • Visualization
